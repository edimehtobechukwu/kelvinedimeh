THE CHALLENGE

The agency lacked an online presence. Users left without requesting quotes and didn't understand the benefits of solar or savings. LumenOne is a startup providing solar solutions. The goal was to modernize the brand online and provide a strong landing experience for lead generation.

THE SOLUTION

Positioned as a trustworthy green energy brand. Features: Scalable Component Design, Clear CTAs, and Sustainability-Driven Visuals. The redesign focused on clarity, using clean typography and spacious layouts to communicate trust and innovation effectively.
