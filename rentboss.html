THE CHALLENGE

Users complained about poor listings, unresponsive landlords, and confusing terms. Needed clear breakdown between short and long rentals and trust indicators. RentBoss connects renters to verified landlords for short and long stays. The goal was to make the booking journey trustworthy, fast, and flexible.

THE SOLUTION

A fast, easy-to-navigate app for all types of rentals. Key features include Smart Filtering, Verified Listings, and Saved Favorites. We focused on simplifying the verification process and creating a "match" system that prioritized compatibility over just location.
